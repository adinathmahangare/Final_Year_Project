Smart HealthCare System

A Brief Introduction

Nowadays, people are becoming less concerned about their health due to hectic life so if there is a smart web app that could help people to analyze their health give them proper advice from experts.

Our project aims to create a web app that can transform the way people take charge of their health while also connecting them to the nearest healthcare providers immediately.This just presents the beginning of this project we will be updating the website with new features.

The health care repository and prediction ecosystem is a powerful tool that leverages Python, AWS, Django, Bootstrap, and machine learning prediction models to manage and analyze vast amounts of healthcare data. It allows healthcare providers to gain insights from patient records, identify patterns, and make informed decisions to improve patient outcomes. The ecosystem's machine learning models can predict potential health risks, provide personalized treatment recommendations, and ultimately improve the quality of healthcare services.
